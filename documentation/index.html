Qiskit Terra Documentation¶

Qiskit (Quantum Information Science Kit) Terra is a Python based set of tools for composing quantum programs at the level of circuits and pulses, optimizing them for the constraints of a particular physical quantum processor, and managing the batched execution of experiments on remote-access backends.
