qiskit.unroll.CircuitBackend¶

class qiskit.unroll.CircuitBackend(basis=None)[ソース]¶

ベースクラス: qiskit.unroll._unrollerbackend.UnrollerBackend

Backend for the unroller that produces a QuantumCircuit.

By default, basis gates are the QX gates.
