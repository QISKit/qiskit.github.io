Functions¶

load Load the internal simulator representation (statevector, probability, density matrix, clifford table) Works on all qubits, and prevents reordering (like barrier).
noise Turn noise on/off in simulator.
save Save the internal simulator representation (statevector, probability, density matrix, clifford table).
snapshot Take a snapshot of the internal simulator representation (statevector, probability, density matrix, clifford table) Works on all qubits, and prevents reordering (like barrier).
wait Apply wait for time t to q.
