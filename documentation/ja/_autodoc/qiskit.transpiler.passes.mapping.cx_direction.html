qiskit.transpiler.passes.mapping.cx_direction module¶

The CX direction rearrenges the direction of the cx nodes to make the circuit compatible with the coupling_map.

Members¶

Classes¶

CXDirection Rearranges the direction of the cx nodes to make the circuit compatible with the directed coupling map.

Reference¶

class qiskit.transpiler.passes.mapping.cx_direction.CXDirection(coupling_map, initial_layout=None)[ソース]¶

ベースクラス: qiskit.transpiler._basepasses.TransformationPass

Rearranges the direction of the cx nodes to make the circuit compatible with the directed coupling map.

It uses this equivalence:

---(+)---      --[H]---.---[H]--
    |      =           |
----.----      --[H]--(+)--[H]--
パラメータ:
  • coupling_map (CouplingMap) -- Directed graph represented a coupling map.
  • initial_layout (Layout) -- The initial layout of the DAG.
__init__(coupling_map, initial_layout=None)[ソース]¶
パラメータ:
  • coupling_map (CouplingMap) -- Directed graph represented a coupling map.
  • initial_layout (Layout) -- The initial layout of the DAG.
run(dag)[ソース]¶

Flips the cx nodes to match the directed coupling map. :param dag: DAG to map. :type dag: DAGCircuit

戻り値:The rearranged dag for the coupling map
戻り値の型:DAGCircuit
Raises:MapperError -- If the circuit cannot be mapped just by flipping the cx nodes.
