qiskit.wrapper.load_qasm_string¶

qiskit.wrapper.load_qasm_string(qasm_string, name=None, basis_gates='id, u0, u1, u2, u3, x, y, z, h, s, sdg, t, tdg, rx, ry, rz, cx, cy, cz, ch, crz, cu1, cu3, swap, ccx, cswap')[ソース]¶

Construct a quantum circuit from a qasm representation (string).

パラメータ:
  • qasm_string (str) -- a string of qasm, or a filename containing qasm.
  • basis_gates (str) -- basis gates for the quantum circuit.
  • name (str or None) -- the name of the quantum circuit after loading qasm text into it. If no name given, assign automatically.
戻り値:

circuit constructed from qasm.
戻り値の型:

QuantumCircuit
Raises:

QISKitError -- if the string is not valid QASM
