qiskit.circuit.compositegate module¶

Composite gate, a container for a sequence of unitary gates.

class CompositeGate(name, params, inverse_name=None)[source]¶

Bases: qiskit.circuit.gate.Gate

Composite gate, a sequence of unitary gates.

append(gate)[source]¶

Attach a gate.

barrier(*qargs)¶

Apply barrier to circuit. If qargs is None, applies to all the qbits. Args is a list of QuantumRegister or single qubits. For QuantumRegister, applies barrier to all the qubits in that register.

c_if(classical, val)[source]¶

Add classical control register.

ccx(ctl1, ctl2, tgt)¶

Apply Toffoli to from ctl1 and ctl2 to tgt.

ch(ctl, tgt)¶

Apply CH from ctl to tgt.

crz(theta, ctl, tgt)¶

Apply crz from ctl to tgt with angle theta.

cswap(ctl, tgt1, tgt2)¶

Apply Fredkin to circuit.

cu1(theta, ctl, tgt)¶

Apply cu1 from ctl to tgt with angle theta.

cu3(theta, phi, lam, ctl, tgt)¶

Apply cu3 from ctl to tgt with angle theta, phi, lam.

cx(ctl, tgt)¶

Apply CX from ctl to tgt.

cx_base(ctl, tgt)¶

Apply CX ctl, tgt.

cy(ctl, tgt)¶

Apply CY to circuit.

cz(ctl, tgt)¶

Apply CZ to circuit.

h(q)¶

Apply H to q.

iden(q)¶

Apply Identity to q.

instruction_list()[source]¶

Return a list of instructions for this CompositeGate.

If the CompositeGate itself contains composites, call this method recursively.

inverse()[source]¶

Invert this gate.

q_if(*qregs)[source]¶

Add controls to this gate.

qasm()[source]¶

Return OPENQASM string.

rx(theta, q)¶

Apply Rx to q.

ry(theta, q)¶

Apply Ry to q.

rz(phi, q)¶

Apply Rz to q.

rzz(theta, qubit1, qubit2)¶

Apply RZZ to circuit.

s(q)¶

Apply S to q.

sdg(q)¶

Apply Sdg to q.

swap(qubit1, qubit2)¶

Apply SWAP from qubit1 to qubit2.

t(q)¶

Apply T to q.

tdg(q)¶

Apply Tdg to q.

u0(m, q)¶

Apply u0 with length m to q.

u1(theta, q)¶

Apply u1 with angle theta to q.

u2(phi, lam, q)¶

Apply u2 to q.

u3(theta, phi, lam, q)¶

Apply u3 to q.

u_base(theta, phi, lam, q)¶

Apply U to q.

unitary(obj, qubits, label=None)¶

Apply u2 to q.

x(q)¶

Apply X to q.

y(q)¶

Apply Y to q.

z(q)¶

Apply Z to q.
