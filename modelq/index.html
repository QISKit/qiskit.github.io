At long last, we are thrilled to unveil the new portable Model Q Composer Keyboard: the optimal companion for the serious quantum programmer.
"No other device on the market offers the flexibility and convenience of the Model Q for quantum programming," said Jay Gambetta, theorist and enthusiastic button-pusher. "What would take me years to develop can now be accomplished in a week...and I no longer suffer from Programmer's Finger Fatigue Syndrome.”
# Join our Slack community
Its clean design and colorful keys finally fill the need our QISKit users have been clamoring for. You can compose a circuit effortlessly and one-handedly.  Featuring the same highly-acclaimed buckling spring action first seen on the legendary IBM Model M, the keys are specially engineered for high-speed composing with low actuation force, yet with mind-blowing rebound speeds and deep strokes to ensure no unintentional entanglement -- of qubits OR of fingers! You will be churning out quantum scores in no time.
